# FINDINGS
The medical condition with the highest revenue is diabetes, with a total sum of $238,539,725.49
Patients within the age range 53-62 visited the hospital most, slightly above age range 33-42. 
Cigna is the most contributing Insurance Provider for the IOTB TECH Hospital.
Most patients were admitted on an Elective basis. 
Patients diagnosed of asthma had the longest stay in the hospital.
Of the medication prescribed to the patients, Lipitor was most prescribed.
Female patients were admitted on emergency more than the male counterparts.
Overall, gender distribution of patients was competitve, although the male patients superceded the female with only 48.
A- is the most common blood group of the patients.
Arthritis is the most common medical condition among the patients diagnosed.

# OBSERVATIONS
Revenue distribution across medical conditions is relatively close.
Middle-aged and older adults recorded high visitation rates.
Insurance providers contributed differently to healthcare financing.
Emergency admissions were lower than elective and urgent admissions.
Medication prescription counts were very similar.
Blood group frequencies were fairly evenly distributed.
Chronic conditions appeared among the highest in both patient count and revenue generation.

# INSIGHTS
Chronic diseases are major drivers of healthcare revenue and admissions.
Older adults require more continuous healthcare services.
Healthcare demand is balanced across genders.
Lipitor and Ibuprofen prescriptions reflect chronic disease treatment trends.
Cigna and Medicare play significant roles in healthcare financing.
Most healthcare interactions involve planned treatments rather than emergencies.

# RECOMMENDATIONS
Increase preventive healthcare programs for chronic diseases.
Develop specialized services for middle-aged and elderly patients.
Strengthen partnerships with major insurance providers.
Improve chronic disease monitoring systems.
Promote early diagnosis and wellness campaigns.
Maintain adequate stock for highly prescribed medications.
